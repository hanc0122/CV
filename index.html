Objective

My passion lies in blending the art and the science of marketing, by drawing on analytics and research to help develop creative solutions for business problems.

My Story

After completing a degree in marketing and psychology, I entered the world of marketing not knowing whether to approach my role with creativity or science. Having now worked in both small business and digital agency, I have learnt that the best approach often requires the ability to wear both hats.

My current role within a digital marketing agency has allowed me to develop a thorough understanding of the technology available across a variety of digital channels. As a result I am able to meet marketing objectives from high-level strategy right down to hands-on execution. I have a drive for learning. What could be called digital FOMO, I have a desire to ensure that no leaf goes unturned in the search for digital marketing opportunities for both my own knowledge and the benefit of those I work with.
